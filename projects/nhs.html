Wye Valley Trust NHS Recruitment Drive

Motion Graphics // Collage Project // Live Brief

Project Brief

For this project, the Wye Valley NHS Trust asked our collage class to produce a campaign for their recruitment drive, specifically the Integrated Care Division, in order to get more people to work for the NHS. I ended up producing a short video advert to help relate the perks for working for the Wye Valley NHS Trust, such as the location. They ended up choosing this video and two others out of our college class to use for their campaign, which was a great achievement for me.

Final Recruitment Video

This video was produced with Adobe Illustrator for the visuals, and Adobe After Effects to do the keyframe animation. To add text and to compile all the animations, I used Adobe Premiere Pro.

Pre-production

Before making the video, I planned out a few ideas I could use with a spider diagram. After settling on a few ideas, I created a simple moodboard to generate ideas for the style and colour I would use. I also made a short animatic in order to get an idea of how I would have transitions within the video, as well as a few basic shot ideas. To see more of the pre-production behind this project, you can visit my Adobe Express page.
